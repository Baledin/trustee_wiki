Immediately following the establishment of an intercounty rural library district the boards of county commissioners of the counties affected shall jointly appoint a board of five or seven trustees for the district in accordance with RCW 27.12.190. The board of trustees shall appoint a librarian for the district. ''[^http://apps.leg.wa.gov/rcw/default.aspx?cite=27.12.130]''
